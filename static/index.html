About 713 Saint Paul Street

Settled on the shores of Blue Hill Bay, with 225 feet of deep-water frontage, the waterside is quite literally a wall of glass. From each room one watches the changing mood of the sea and sky, yet is cozy and deeply insulated by the solid construction and southern exposure.

While densely built, this house has a light, airy, warm feeling about it. It is 3200 square feet with large volume spaces, three bedrooms, two and a half baths, open kitchen and dining room, double height living room with large soapstone fireplace, hang space (TV room), library, mud-room, laundry, two car garage with heated slab, extensive covered porches and decks, and two outdoor showers (both with ocean views).

Second floor master suite has amazing 'pilot house' views, walk-in closet with attached laundry room, huge bathroom including a shower without walls, oversize soaking bathtub, 'partner' double sink, bidet, extensive glass front cabinets, dressing table, and an integral exterior deck with shower looking out onto the bay.

Designed by a LEED accredited architect, this modern home utilizes traditional materials in a non-traditional way, and is boldly out front in ecological and energy efficient design.
