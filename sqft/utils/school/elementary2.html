Maryland School Assessment (MSA)

The Maryland School Assessment is an annual assessment program that tests grades 3 through 8 in reading and mathematics. Two tests are also required at the high school level to measure reading and mathematics. The MSA results are used in the calculation of whether a school met the AYP target. Science scores are represented here for the first time at grades 5 and 8 and the high school level but are not a part of AYP.
